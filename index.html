HEALTH INSURANCE RISK CALCULATOR


INSTRUCTIONS

The following program is useful for classifying health insurance with respect to risk.

The risk of said individual is determined by their health as calculated by their body-mass index, blood pressure, age, and family history of disease.

To determine your total risk score, open the drop-down boxes and select the appropriate item for each question.

Then click on the button labeled 'Total Calculated Health Risk' to see your result.
